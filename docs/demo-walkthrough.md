# Demo Walkthrough

## Best path for an application demo

1. Start in the operations view and point at the KPI row to frame the product as a control tower, not a passive tracker.
2. Open the global shipment board and filter between ocean, air, trucking, and parcel to show the multi-modal story.
3. Select `OCN-447219` and walk through the timeline, event feed, map card, and recommended actions.
4. Call out the exception heatmap and SLA widgets to show customer and profitability impact, not just status updates.
5. Open the MCP copilot card and use the canned prompts to answer “Why is this shipment red?” and “Show loads at risk in the next 24 hours.”
6. Switch to the partner portal and rotate roles between `customer`, `partner`, and `ops_analyst` to show permission-aware self-service.
7. Finish in `docs/openapi.yaml`, `config/workflows`, and `config/app-modules` to connect the UI back to CXTMS-native primitives.

## Commercial pitch

CXTMS Control Tower OS moves the platform from system of record toward system of orchestration by combining prediction, workflow automation, partner APIs, and external collaboration into one layer.
